Paradise Valley Landscape Design & Build.

A boutique landscape studio for Paradise Valley compounds. Custom design, travertine and paver hardscape, resort-style backyards, and Discreet Mode quiet maintenance — built to the standards PV properties are known for.

Built for PV

Compound-Scale Work Without Compound-Scale Disruption.

Paradise Valley is not a neighborhood — it is a town of roughly 13,000 residents with some of the largest private lots in the Valley. Multi-acre compounds, horse properties, walled estates tucked into the base of Camelback and Mummy Mountain — the scale of this work is different, and so is the pace at which it has to be executed.

We have worked inside PV for over a decade. The crews know the town's conditions: long driveways that need staging plans, guard gates, neighbors who hear everything, and view easements that determine where a ramada can and cannot go. Every project we run here is scoped and sequenced so the property stays livable the entire time we are on it.

Services in Paradise Valley

Everything a Compound Needs, Under One Roof.

Estate Landscape Design

Full-property master planning for multi-acre lots. Pool surrounds, guest casitas, circulation, privacy planting, and view preservation for Camelback, Mummy, and Praying Monk corridors.

Hardscape & Stonework

Travertine, paver, porcelain, and natural stone patios, pool decks, driveways, and walkways. Retaining walls, seat walls, and courtyards built to last decades — and to read as original to the architecture.

Resort Backyards

Outdoor kitchens, fire features, ramadas, pergolas, putting greens, and pool-deck integration. Designed so the yard reads as a continuation of the home, not an add-on.

Smart Irrigation

Zone-by-zone design for large lots with varied sun exposure. Smart controllers with weather-based scheduling, drip systems for desert-adapted plantings, and seasonal tuning.

Outdoor Lighting

Low-voltage lighting tuned to architectural features, specimen trees, and pathways. Fixtures selected to compliment the home's palette — bronze, iron, copper, or painted steel.

Estate Maintenance

Weekly or bi-weekly care with the same crew, same day. Pruning, seasonal color rotations, irrigation checks, pressure washing, and everything in between — kept to the standard the project was installed at.

Discreet Mode

Quiet Service for Privacy-Forward Properties.

Paradise Valley residents value privacy and quiet. Gas crews with blowers, trimmers, and idling trucks pulling up at dawn is not what this town signed up for. That is why we built Discreet Mode — our signature quiet-service tier.

  • All-electric equipment. Battery blowers, mowers, and trimmers — no two-stroke noise, no exhaust. Your meeting or morning call will not know we are there.
  • Briefed, uniformed crews. No chatter, no idling, no radios. Foremen know the household's routines and work around them.
  • Unmarked vehicles available. On request, we arrive without signage.
  • Scheduling around your calendar. Service chosen around work-from-home hours, travel, hosts, and events.

Discreet Mode is available for all weekly maintenance clients in Paradise Valley and the Camelback Corridor.

How We Work

A Process Built for Properties This Size.

PV projects start with an on-site walk-through — not a sales pitch. We listen more than we talk. From there, we produce a written scope and a phased build plan so the property remains usable through installation. On larger projects, we sequence the work so pools, hosting areas, and primary outdoor spaces come online first, with finish phases following.

Construction runs with one foreman, one point of contact, and a single pass at finish. When the build is done, our maintenance crew takes over to the same standards. There is no quality drop-off at handoff — the same company that built it is the one keeping it up.
