Silverleaf at DC Ranch

Luxury Landscape Design in Silverleaf.

Estate-standard craftsmanship for one of North Scottsdale's most private communities. Travertine and paver hardscape, resort-style backyards, and Discreet Mode quiet maintenance — executed to the architectural standards Silverleaf homes are built on.

Why Silverleaf

Landscape Work That Matches the Caliber of Your Home.

Silverleaf sits inside the gates of DC Ranch in North Scottsdale — a master-planned community where homes routinely run into the eight figures and architecture spans Mediterranean, Tuscan, Spanish Colonial, and Santa Barbara vernaculars. The landscape is expected to live up to what sits on the lot. That is a narrow brief, and it is the one we have been quietly working to for over a decade.

Most landscape companies are built to move volume. Nature Formed is not. We operate as a boutique studio — small crews, long-tenured foremen, materials sourced to match the tone of the house, and a pace that favors finish quality over throughput. That is the only way work of this grade gets built.

What We Build in Silverleaf

Full-Scope Estate Landscape Services.

Estate Landscape Design

Site-specific master planning — circulation, view corridors, shade structures, pool surrounds, and planting palettes keyed to the home's exterior material language. Plans are rendered clearly enough to run through a DC Ranch design review without surprises.

Travertine & Paver Hardscape

Custom patios, pool decks, driveways, walkways, and seat walls in travertine, porcelain, natural stone, and premium pavers. Every joint, bond pattern, and edge detail is laid to finish-carpentry tolerances.

Resort-Style Backyards

Pool decking, outdoor kitchens, fire features, ramadas, putting greens, and shade-structure integration. Built to feel like the backyard has always been part of the house.

Smart Irrigation & Drip

Zone-by-zone irrigation design, drip for desert-adapted plants, smart controllers with weather-responsive scheduling, and seasonal adjustments tuned to Silverleaf's elevation and exposure.

Low-Voltage Outdoor Lighting

Path, uplight, downlight, accent, and security lighting designed to compliment architectural features at night — never overlit, never harsh. Fixtures matched to the home's finish palette.

Estate Maintenance

Weekly or bi-weekly stewardship with the same crew, same day, same standard. Pruning, seasonal color rotations, irrigation checks, pressure washing, and anything else the property needs — without having to ask twice.

Discreet Mode

Service That Respects The Way Silverleaf Lives.

Silverleaf residents work from home, host guests, and value privacy. Loud gas crews pulling onto the driveway at 7 AM does not fit the way these properties are lived in. That is why we built Discreet Mode — our signature quiet-service tier.

  • 100% professional electric equipment. No gas engines, no two-stroke noise. Work happens without disrupting a meeting or a morning coffee on the patio.
  • Uniformed, briefed crews. Quiet arrival, no chatter, no engine idling. Foremen know the property and the household's preferences.
  • Unmarked vehicles available. On request, we arrive without signage — standard practice for properties where privacy is part of the product.
  • Scheduled around your calendar. Service windows chosen around work-from-home hours, travel, and events.

Discreet Mode is available for all weekly maintenance clients in Silverleaf and the broader DC Ranch community.

How We Work

From First Walk-Through to Weekly Stewardship.

Most Silverleaf projects begin with a private on-site walk-through. We listen more than we talk — the goal of the first visit is to understand the home, the way you use it, and the standards you expect. From there, we produce a written scope and a phased plan. Where the work touches anything reviewable by the HOA, we handle the documentation.

Construction runs with one foreman on site, one number to call, and a single pass at finish. After installation, we hand the property to our maintenance crew on the same standards that built it. No handoff gap. No quality drop in month three.
