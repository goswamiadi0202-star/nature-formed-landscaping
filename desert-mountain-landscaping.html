Desert Mountain · Far North Scottsdale

Desert Mountain Landscape Design & Estate Care.

Boutique landscape studio for one of Arizona's most private communities. Estate design, travertine hardscape, resort backyards, and Discreet Mode quiet maintenance — executed with the discretion Desert Mountain residents expect.

A Different Scale of Quiet

Landscape Work That Matches the Community.

Desert Mountain is one of the most exclusive residential communities in the American Southwest — a guard-gated golf and residential club perched above 2,500 feet in the high Sonoran desert. Homes range from restrained desert contemporary to multi-acre mountain estates. Privacy, discretion, and quiet are not amenities here. They are the entire product.

That is why the landscape work that fits Desert Mountain is not the work that moves through volume shops in South Scottsdale. It is slower, more considered, and quieter in both execution and aesthetic. We have built to that brief for over a decade.

Services in Desert Mountain

Estate-Grade, End To End.

Estate Landscape Design

Full-property master planning for multi-acre mountain lots. Driveways, motor courts, guest casitas, and pool surrounds planned around preserved desert and long-range mountain views.

Travertine & Stone

Travertine, paver, natural stone, and cast concrete hardscape. Patios, pool decks, courtyards, walkways, and retaining walls detailed to tolerances that hold up against Desert Mountain's architectural standard.

Resort Backyards

Pool-deck integration, ramadas, outdoor kitchens, fire features, and putting greens — planned to frame the view and blend with the preserved desert around the home.

Smart Irrigation

Drip-based systems for desert plantings, weather-responsive controllers, and seasonal scheduling tuned to Desert Mountain's elevation, exposure, and microclimate.

Dark-Sky Lighting

Low-voltage, downward-aimed, warm-temperature lighting. Designed to reveal the architecture and specimen plantings at night without compromising the community's dark-sky character.

Estate Maintenance

Weekly stewardship by the same crew, same day. Hand-pruning of desert natives, seasonal irrigation tuning, pressure washing, and everything else an estate needs — kept to the standard the property was built to.

Discreet Mode

Service Built for How Desert Mountain Lives.

Desert Mountain is a community where privacy and silence are non-negotiable. That is precisely the standard Discreet Mode was designed for.

  • 100% professional electric equipment. No gas engines, no two-stroke noise, no exhaust drifting across your patio.
  • Uniformed, briefed crews. No chatter, no radios, no idling trucks.
  • Unmarked vehicles available. On request, we arrive without signage.
  • Scheduling around your calendar. Service chosen around household routines, travel, and guests.

Discreet Mode is available for all weekly maintenance clients inside Desert Mountain, and is standard practice on every estate we care for in the community.

How We Work

Considered First Visit. Precise Execution.

Desert Mountain projects begin with an on-site walk-through — not a pitch. We listen, walk the lot, and take the time to understand how the household actually uses the property. From there, we produce a written scope and a phased plan. Where work requires community approval, we handle the documentation.

Construction runs with one foreman, one point of contact, and a single pass at finish. When the build is complete, maintenance hands off to the same standard — and with the same crew who knows the property. No quality drop-off. No stranger showing up on the driveway six months later.
