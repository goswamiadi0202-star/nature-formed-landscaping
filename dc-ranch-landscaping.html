DC Ranch Standards

Work That Passes Design Review The First Time.

DC Ranch is a master-planned community stretching across the foothills of North Scottsdale, bounded by the McDowell Mountains and the Sonoran Desert preserve. Its villages — from the Country Club to Horseshoe Canyon to gated Silverleaf — share a set of design standards administered by an active Design Review process. We have worked inside those standards for over a decade.

That means our plans arrive in a format DC Ranch is already used to reviewing: clear site diagrams, plant palettes within approved lists, hardscape materials keyed to the home's exterior, and lighting specs that respect the community's dark-sky sensibilities. Our goal is to get you approved on the first pass — without the surprise revisions that cost homeowners weeks.

Services in DC Ranch

Every Village. Every Scope. One Team.

Landscape Design

Property-specific plans keyed to your village's design language — Country Club's contemporary desert, Homestead's traditional Southwest, Horseshoe Canyon's mountain modern, Silverleaf's Mediterranean and Tuscan vernaculars.

Travertine & Paver Hardscape

Patios, pool decks, driveways, walkways, seat walls, and retaining walls in travertine, paver, and natural stone. Detailed to tolerances expected in homes at this level.

Resort Backyards

Pool-deck builds, ramadas, outdoor kitchens, fire features, and putting greens. Integrated so the backyard feels like a continuation of the home, not an afterthought.

Smart Irrigation

Weather-responsive controllers, drip for desert plantings, and zone design tuned to each village's soil, slope, and sun exposure. Retrofits and new installs.

Low-Voltage Lighting

Accent, path, uplight, and downlight design specified to DC Ranch's dark-sky guidelines. Fixtures matched to the home's finish palette.

Estate Maintenance

Weekly, bi-weekly, and seasonal stewardship — with the same crew, same day, every week. Pruning, color rotations, irrigation checks, pressure washing, and property-wide hygiene.

Discreet Mode

Service That Matches DC Ranch's Quiet.

DC Ranch is a residential-forward community where homeowners work from home, host, and value quiet mornings. Loud gas crews do not fit the way these homes are lived in. That is why we built Discreet Mode — our signature quiet-service tier.

  • 100% professional electric equipment. No gas engines, no two-stroke whine. Dawn service is actually quiet.
  • Briefed, uniformed crews. No chatter, no radios, no idling. Foremen know the property and the household.
  • Unmarked vehicles available. On request, we arrive without signage.
  • Calendar-aware scheduling. Service chosen around work-from-home hours, travel, and events.

Discreet Mode is available for all weekly maintenance clients across DC Ranch — Country Club, Homestead, Horseshoe Canyon, and Silverleaf.

How We Work

One Team. Built-To-Maintained, Without a Handoff.

We begin with an on-site walk-through. From there, we produce a written scope, a phased plan, and any documentation DC Ranch Design Review needs. Construction runs with one foreman, one point of contact, and a single pass at finish. When the build is done, our maintenance crew takes over — at the same standard that built the property.

The result: the same company that designed the yard is the one keeping it. No third-party maintenance crew showing up months later and undoing the work.
